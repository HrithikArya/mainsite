About us

Quizzers' Club Manit (QCM) is the official quizzing society of Maulana Azad Institute of Technology Bhopal. QCM is formed by a bunch of quizzing prodigies of Manit in Feb 2013. We are a group of enthusiastic quizzers, executives and designers. The purpose of the society is to promote quizzing, group discussion, debate and to increase the level of general awareness amongst the student community. Quizzers' club conducts various successful events throughout the year in which students from different colleges of India participate.

The main events organized by Quizzers' Club Manit are Manthan, TQQW, Quizzotica and Vihaan. We try to bring like-minded quizzing enthusiasts together on a platform for mutual sharing of ideas and probing the globe of knowledge. Quizzers' Club Manit provides opportunity to the students to show their quizzing skills. We are planning to conduct more quizzing events online due to this pandemic and are also planning on conducting inter college competitions. We post questions and intresting facts on our social media pages daily to enhance everyone's learning and curiosity.

ABHIGYAN

To test your mettle in quizzing in a completely unorthodox manner, QCM is back with its most-awaited and largest ever online event, ABHIGYAN’ 20 to unite ardent quizzing enthusiasts from all parts of the country. There are cash prizes worth Rs 9k and more and every participant will be awarded a participation certificate. The preliminary round of ABHIGYAN’20 is set to be held on Dare2Compete (D2C) this 22nd October.

VIHAAN

VIHAAN is QCM's opening event for session( It take place even before fresher's evening). The event is organized for the first year students to participate , learn and to let them know what awaits them in future. VIHAAN is a platform for the students, amateurs, quizzers and newcomers to mettle in quizzing.

QUIZZOTICA

QUIZZOTICA is QCM's event in TECHNOSEARCH ( largest technical fest in central India). It has two rounds. First being a group competition to test knowledge and creativity. Second round is a group competition with different sub-rounds. The event aims to make quizing more exciting, entertaining and challenging.

MATHAN

Manthan is a QCM's flagship event. Its first round consists of quizzes in various colleges across the country ( our team vistited 15 colleges in 2018).Second round is a quiz competition among the quizmasters of state. Each students represents their college which leads to better competition and bragging rights.

TQQM

The Quarantine Quizzing Week (TQQW) our longest and first online event till date. As Manthan'20 got cancelled due to this covid-19 pandemic it is replaced by TQQW. In TQQW we organized quiz on the daily basis for a week on topics like history, sports, business etc. The winners were awarded with cash prizes and certificates.
